Commands List

Fun Commands

  • blackjack

    ・Aliases: bj
    ・Syntax: C.blackjack [bet] {opponent}
    ・Cooldown: 15 seconds
    ・You can bet from $ 100 to $ 100,000
    ・You can either let your friend deal the cards or let Cartero pick a dealer.

    How to play blackjack?
    ・Get the total as close to 21 as possible, without going over 21.
    ・An Ace is worth either 1 or 11 depending upon the situation.
    ・Every number card is worth the number itself.
    ・All the face cards are worth 10.
    ・The dealer will deal two cards initially.
    ・You can choose to either HIT (👊) or STAND(🛑).
    ・On choosing HIT, the dealer will deal another card to you.
    ・While on choosing STAND, you withdraw yourself from dealing any more cards.
    ・You can HIT for a maximum of three times.
    ・Don't forget to respond within 60 seconds, else it will be considered as STAND.

  • 8ball

    ・Aliases: 8
    ・Syntax: C.8ball [question]
    ・Chooses a random answer from a ball with 10 affirmative, 5 non-committal, and 5 negative answers .

  • choose

    ・Aliases: choice, c
    ・Syntax: C.choose [option A] [option B]
    ・Choose between option A and option B.

  • roast

    ・Syntax: C.roast [user]
    ・Roast the mentioned user.

Moderation Commands

  • purge

    ・Aliases: clear
    ・Syntax: C.purge [amount (Default: 1)]
    ・Requires Manage Messages permission.
    ・Used to purge messages in a text channel.

  • add emoji

    ・Syntax: C.add emoji [emoji] {name}
    ・Requires Manage Emojis permission.
    ・Used to add emojis to the server.

  • delete emoji

    ・Syntax: C.delete emoji [emoji]
    ・Requires Manage Emojis permission.
    ・Used to delete emojis from the server.

  • massmove

    ・Aliases: mm
    ・Syntax: C.massmove [current channel id] [new channel id]
    ・Requires Move Members permission.
    ・Moves all the members from current voice channel to new voice channel.

  • mute

    ・Syntax: C.mute [user] {reason (Default: None)}
    ・Requires Manage Messages permission.
    ・Assigns 'Muted' role and sends a message with the reason to the mentioned user.

  • unmute

    ・Syntax: C.unmute [user]
    ・Requires Manage Messages permission.
    ・Removes 'Muted' role and sends a message to the mentioned user.

  • kick

    ・Syntax: C.kick [user] {reason (Default: None)}
    ・Requires Kick Members permission.
    ・Kicks the mentioned user.

  • ban

    ・Syntax: C.ban [user] {reason (Default: None)}
    ・Requires Ban Members permission.
    ・Bans the mentioned user.

  • unban

    ・Syntax: C.unban [user] {reason (Default: None)}
    ・Requires Ban Members permission.
    ・Unbans the mentioned user.

  • set prefix

    ・Syntax: [current prefix]set prefix [new prefix]
    ・Requires Administrator permission.
    ・Changes the server prefix for Cartero.

  • set default

    ・Syntax: [current prefix]set default
    ・Requires Administrator permission.
    ・Resets the server prefix for Cartero.

  • toggle anonymous

    ・Aliases: toggle anon
    ・Syntax: C.toggle anonymous
    ・Only owner can use the command.
    ・Toggle between anonymous owner displayed in C.guildinfo
